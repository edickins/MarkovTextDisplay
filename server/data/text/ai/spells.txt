### Spell for Clarity of Mind

**Ingredients:**
- 3 dragon's teeth
- 1 vial of moonlit dew
- 2 drops of phoenix feather essence
- 1 silver hair from a unicorn

**Instructions:**
1. Under the light of a full moon, gather your ingredients in a quiet space.
2. Crush the dragon's teeth into a fine powder and place them in a small bowl.
3. Add the moonlit dew and mix until a paste forms.
4. Carefully add the phoenix feather essence, stirring clockwise to infuse the mixture with clarity.
5. Finally, weave the silver hair into the paste, chanting the incantation: "By the light of the moon and the fire of the phoenix, grant me clarity, let my thoughts be seamless."
6. Apply the mixture to your forehead and meditate for ten minutes, visualizing a bright light illuminating your mind.

### Potion of Protection

**Ingredients:**
- 5 frog's eyes
- 1 pinch of powdered obsidian
- 2 sprigs of rosemary
- 1 drop of dragon's blood resin

**Instructions:**
1. In a cauldron, bring a cup of spring water to a gentle boil.
2. Add the frog's eyes and stir counterclockwise while reciting: "Eyes of the unseen, protect me from harm."
3. Sprinkle in the powdered obsidian and continue stirring until the mixture turns a deep black.
4. Add the rosemary, allowing it to steep for five minutes, then strain the potion into a glass vial.
5. Finally, add a drop of dragon's blood resin, sealing the vial tightly.
6. Carry the potion with you or anoint your doorways to ward off negative energies.
